About Us

he Institute of Technology was founded in 1995 with a mission to provide high-quality education and training to students from all over the world. Today, we are one of the leading technology institutes in the world, with a reputation for academic excellence and innovation. Our students come from over 15 different schools and colleges. We offer a wide range of courses. Our faculty are renowned experts in their fields, and they are committed to providing our students with the best possible education. We are located in a beautiful campus in the heart of Berhampur. Our campus is home to state-of-the-art facilities, including a library, laboratories, and classrooms. We also have a strong student life, with a variety of clubs, organizations, and activities for students to participate in. We are committed to using our knowledge and resources to make a positive impact on the world.
